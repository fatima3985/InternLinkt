How InternLinkt Works

Create Your Profile

Students build comprehensive profiles showcasing their skills, experience, and career aspirations. Companies create detailed company profiles highlighting their culture and opportunities.

Discover Opportunities

Our intelligent matching algorithm connects students with relevant internships based on skills, location, and preferences. Companies can browse student profiles to find perfect candidates.

Apply & Connect

Students apply with one-click applications including resume and cover letter. Companies review applications and connect directly with promising candidates.

Land Your Dream Role

Track application status, schedule interviews, and secure your internship. Our platform streamlines the entire process from application to placement.
